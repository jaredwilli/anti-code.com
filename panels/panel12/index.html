For The Port Candle, I developed websites for the company's blog and also an eCommerce site for selling their products online. I also designed and created the company's entire brand and online presence.

  • The shopping cart web site was developed with WordPress and a shop plugin. There is a custom post type for the products, and also one for special promotions that are offered occassionally.

  • The products are displayed in two different ways, you can change the view to see both a grid view and list view.

  • The blog has a custom post slider for teasing products, news or other announcements.
